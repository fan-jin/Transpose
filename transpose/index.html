About

On May 15, 2016 our group was brought together by Venture for Canada on Queens Campus. Venture for Canada is a not-for-profit that recruits, trains, and supports recent graduates who are pursuing entrepreneurial careers. At transpose we all believe there is a need for new processes for students and professors to create a conversation moving towards to bettering Canadian University. How it works

PRE COURSE SURVEY

After registering for courses students have the opportunity to fill out a survey that will give professors insight on the different learning styles of the classroom.

FORMATIVE EVALUATION

The first course evaluation will occur during the midterm period and will offer the professor ideas on different engagement strategies for those may be struggling in the course.

SUMMATIVE EVALUATION

SUMMATIVE EVALUATION The final course evaluation will occur at the end of the semester. This portion will give students to give an overall review of the course and to reflect on the professors adjustments made based on the pre course and formative evaluation.

CONTINUOUS PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Transpose will continue to support professors throughout their career with long term tracking as well as recognition for outstanding work in the classroom.
